Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open the Settings app, tap your name, then "Subscriptions" to view, change, or cancel your Innerbloom subscription.
2. How does Innerbloom protect my privacy?
We take your privacy seriously. Please see our Privacy Policy for details on how your data is collected, used, and protected.
3. I purchased a subscription but Pro features are locked. What should I do?
Try restoring your purchase from the app's settings. If the issue persists, contact us at inimitabletech@gmail.com with your Apple receipt.
4. Can I get a refund for my subscription?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for your purchase.
5. What features are included in Innerbloom Pro?
Pro unlocks unlimited access to all sessions, advanced analytics, exclusive meditations, unlimited journaling, and more. See the app for full details.
